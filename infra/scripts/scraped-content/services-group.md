## Overcoming the need to fix others

We respond to the lack of unconditional love in the family dynamic by wanting to make everything ok. We want to be liked and to please others to avoid controversy. We learn as a small child it is safe to fix others problems and difficulties and we carry this on into adulthood. So how do we stop it? Join us on this 4 week challenge of overcoming fixing others, learn how to respond in a loving way and free up time and energy for new dreams and desire. Through this 4 session series repatterning class, part coaching part class, find out what is underneath this behaviour and move into a new way of being.
